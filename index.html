🔑 Key Findings

  • ✨ Youth unemployment remains significantly higher than overall unemployment, with rates around 60.2%.
  • 📍 Provincial disparities are persistent — certain provinces consistently show higher unemployment rates.
  • 📉 Long-term unemployment continues to weigh heavily on economic growth, reducing labour force participation to 59%.
  • ⚡ Surprising trend: Despite national shifts, youth unemployment barely improves — a structural issue.
  • 💡 What people should know: Tackling unemployment requires youth-focused programs, provincial interventions, and strategies for long-term unemployed workers.
